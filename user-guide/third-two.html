User Guide for Writers of Reference Letters

You will be on the Refs page once you have logged into AcademicJobsOnline.Org. The actions available to you are presented as *links* across the top of the page. If you are unfamiliar with what you can do, please first read the information on getting started as a AcademicJobsOnline reference letter writer. If you have any questions, please contact us.

  • Refs .. is where you create, submit, or update your reference letters for an applicant.
  • Jobs .. allows you to view the current job list.
  • Employers .. allows you to view the current list of Employers.
  • Under Help
    • FAQs refer to Frequently Asked Questions in your area.
    • Contact Us sends us an email with your question or comment.
    • New Password changes your password.
    • Change Email allows you to change the email address for your login as "Job Applicant".
  • Logout .. logs you out of your current AcademicJobsOnline.org account.
