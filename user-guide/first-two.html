Costs and Payment

The annual fee and all current pricing options for employers using the system can be found by clicking on the Membership Types & Fees on the home page of AcademicJobsOnline.org.

Make sure that you understand the differences between the Departmental Membership and the Institutional Membership. A departmental account can be a part of a university-wide institutional membership.

New Employers will receive an email with payment instructions and new account access code once the account has been verified.

Currently enrolled employers can renew their accounts by first logging in and then clicking on the Renewal item under Admin in the menu links.

There is no charge for applicants to use AcademicJobsOnline.org.
