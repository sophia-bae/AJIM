User Guide for Departmental AcademicJobsOnline Administrators

You will be on the List page once you have logged in to AcademicJobsOnline.Org. The actions available to you are presented as *links* across the top of the page. If you are unfamiliar with what you can do, please first read the information on getting started as an Institutional AcademicJobsOnline manager. If you have any questions, please contact us.

  • Lists .. list applications received for all current jobs. Also allows you to view and update applications.
  • Search .. for applicants using various criteria. You can access all applicants who have applied for at least one of your open positions.
  • Jobs .. allows employers to edit their jobs with such updates as setting a deadline and updating listings as withdrawn, filled or finished. The complete current public job list can also be viewed.
  • Config .. allows you to edit the information you entered about your institution and define parameters for job postings in the group.
  • Renewal .. allows you to renew your membership for AcademicJobsOnline. Just go to Admin > Renewal, select the option you like and submit.
  • Members .. allows you to view, edit, or add others to AcademicJobsOnline with access as a Faculty Member, EEO Office Login, or another Group Database Manger in your group.
  • Committees .. set up search committees to limit access to individual jobs to selected faculty.
  • NewJob .. allows you to post an open position.
  • NoCover .. list out all applicants without a cover sheet, with options to contact the applicant or complete the cover sheet for the applicants yourself.
  • NewApp .. allows employers to enter the name and email address of those applicants whose materials came in on paper.
  • asFaculty .. allows you to switch back and forth between Administrator and Faculty roles.
  • Shared Notes .. share common notes and files among members.
  • Labels .. is for exporting basic applicant data, etc.. It downloads data so that you can produce labels, lists or merge letters from your own software.
  • News .. is to post announcements for particular sets of users within your dept or your applicants which will appear on their main screens when they log in.
  • Email .. allows you to send emails to a select group of applicants, group managers or faculty at one time.
  • EOE .. shows you a summary report based on the EOE questions you may have added to all applications on the Config screen.
  • Snapshots .. allows you to save and/or download, in one zip file, all current application materials.
  • Wanted .. allows you to view all of the publicly available applicants.
  • Under Help
    • FAQs refer to Frequently Asked Questions in your area.
    • Contact Us sends us an email with your question or comment.
    • New Password changes your password.
    • Change Email allows you to change the email address for your login as "Job Applicant".
  • Logout .. logs you out of your current AcademicJobsOnline.org account.
