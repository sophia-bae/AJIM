The Institute of Physics at the Academia Sinica invites applications from outstanding experimental physicists for a tenure-track faculty position in the Medium and High-energy Physics (MHEP) Group to enhance and develop its research programs and to initiate and lead new directions. The position is open for all ranks, including assistant, associate, and full research fellow (equivalent to university faculty). Academia Sinica is the leading basic research institution in Taiwan, government funded and made up of an increasingly international community of scholars. The MHEP experimental groups pursue experimental, instrumentation programs on collider, hadron structure, cosmic rays, neutrinos, dark matter, beyond the standard model and gravitational physics, with strong computing support. We participate in international collaborations like AMS/ISS, CDEX/CJPL, ATLAS/LHC, SeaQuest/FNAL, COMPASS/CERN, E50/J-PARC, KAGRA, and LIGO, while leading a domestic TEXONO experiment on reactor neutrinos. The experimental programs are complemented by active theory programs covering a broad spectrum of particle, astro-particle, and cosmology physics.
